ABOUT THIS ALBUM

Concept

I created a prototype called Emotion Dice, is an interactive web that users can roll a dice to trigger a piece of music that represents a specific emotion, users not just directly control what they want to listen, but they explore the music through a creative way with randomness and playfulness. By connecting the dice with emotions, this prototype becomes like a self-discover tool, the randomness mirrors the unpredictability of human emotions themselves, users can feel engaging through interact and experience the different emotions of music. This prototype follows the creative prompt of skeuomorphism, the design makes users feel like they are playing with the real dice, which let the interface intuitive and evoke emotional engagement through the experience.
