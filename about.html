About Yehezkel Raz

Yehezkel Raz is an architect of musical minimalism, where intimate piano whispers intertwine with rich, layered soundscapes. His work is a delicate balance of silence and resonance, crafting sonic worlds that feel both profound and unassuming.

Described by fans as a maestro of musical alchemy—a wizard—Yehezkel operates at the crossroads of the tangible and the ethereal, the melodic and the abstract. His live performances are immersive journeys into uncharted realms, where each note draws listeners deeper into a space where soundscapes shift between hushed intimacy and symphonic grandeur. His music transcends borders, resonating with a diverse global audience.

With millions of monthly listeners and recent releases propelling him to the forefront of his musical niche, Yehezkel is gaining massive international recognition. As he prepares to release new music in 2025 and embark on a worldwide tour, his influence continues to expand.
