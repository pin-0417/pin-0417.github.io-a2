About This Album

Release:2019 Genre:classical

Yehezkel Raz presents an album of pristine Classical Piano compositions played masterfully, delving into a vast array of moods, including Dramatic, Uplifting, Serious, Carefree and Love. It's obvious after the first few seconds that we're listening to pure, unbridled talent and emotion at the highest level.
